# 📊 Executive Summary

Marc Lou is a solo entrepreneur generating $77,000 per month through a portfolio of 35 startups. Rather than focusing on a single venture, Marc embraces a "ship everything" mentality—launching dozens of small projects, accepting a 5% hit rate, and letting the mathematics of volume drive his success. His philosophy centers on deep work, consistent daily routines, and the relentless pursuit of shipping new products. Through his "keep rolling the dice" approach, he has built a sustainable solopreneur lifestyle by treating startup creation as a numbers game rather than a one-shot bet.

# 🧠 Frameworks & Lessons

---

## Mental Models

> 🧩 **Ship to validate** — The only way to validate an idea is to ship it with a buy button. Don't spend months perfecting—launch and let the market decide.

> 🧩 **Deep work protection** — Block 4-6 hours daily for deep work. No phone, no email, no social media. This is what got him here and must be protected.

> 🧩 **The dice roll math** — With a 5% hit rate, the strategy isn't to get one idea right—it's to roll the dice enough times. Ship 35, get 1-2 winners.

> 🧩 **Sleep as competitive advantage** — Consistent 9 PM bedtime and 30-60 min wind-down routine leads to emotional stability and super-focused days.

> 🧩 **One-time fear** — The fear of showing your creation to the world is a one-time event. Kill 80% of it by doing it once.
