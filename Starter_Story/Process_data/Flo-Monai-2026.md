# 🚀 Flo: Monai

**Founder:** Flo | **Company:** Monai | **Revenue:** $35K MRR

---

# 📊 Executive Summary

Flo is a solo developer from Germany who built Monai, an AI-powered expense tracking app. After struggling for 18 months to grow his app to just $300 MRR through solo efforts, he partnered with one Colombian content creator and achieved a 10,000% growth rate - scaling from $300 to $35K MRR in just over a year. This story demonstrates that distribution matters more than the product itself, and that one strategic partnership can be more effective than trying to scale through multiple channels or paid ads. Flo's approach of giving equity to his influencer partner created aligned incentives that led to high-quality, story-driven content that converted at exceptional rates.

# 🚀 The Product & Value Prop

---

## What Was Built

> 🏗️ **Monai** - A minimalistic AI-powered expense tracking app that makes recording expenses frictionless through voice input and Apple Pay automations. Unlike bloated traditional expense apps, Monai focuses on simplicity and ease of use.

---

## The Gap

> 💡 **Pain point:** Existing expense tracking apps were too cumbersome - users constantly quit using them because the friction of manual entry wasn't worth the effort. Flo solved this by using AI to enable voice-based expense entry and automatic transaction import via Apple Pay.

---

## Unfair Advantage

> 🏆 **Minimalist approach in a crowded market:** While budget tracking apps are ubiquitous, Monai stands out through its extreme simplicity - no tabs, no complex navigation, just the expense distribution and list. Combined with AI features that understand natural language for categorization and can generate financial insights.

# 🎯 Distribution Playbook

---

## First 100 Users

> 🎣 **Solo distribution struggle:** For 18 months, Flo tried everything himself - organic social, Reddit, TikTok - and only managed $300 MRR. The app was good but nobody was seeing it. This is the classic developer trap: building something people need but not finding a way to reach them.

---

## Scaling Engine

> 📈 **Primary Channel:** Influencer partnership with profit-sharing model
> - Partner is a Colombian content creator (not US-based)
> - 3 high-quality videos per month (quality over quantity)
> - First video 10xed MRR within a week, hit $8K within a month
> - One viral video got 1.7M views and added ~5K MRR
> 
> 📊 **Partnership Structure:**
> - Profit-sharing + fixed monthly retainer
> - Influencer has skin in the game (payout is mostly profit-based)
> - He's incentivized to think about what's best for the app, not just deliver videos
> - He's responsible for video ideas -Flo doesn't need to be a content creator
> 
> 🔄 **Secondary Channels:** Started experimenting with Meta ads using the creator's videos as ad creative - seeing great early results

---

## Influencer Marketing Playbook (Flo's 5-Step System)

**Step 1: Find aligned partners**
- Influencer's lifestyle, tone, and audience must match your product
- Look for: personality, charisma, connects with viewers, replies to comments
- Tech + lifestyle blend (not pure tech influencer) works best

**Step 2: Warm up the relationship**
- Follow them beforehand, engage with their content
- Shows you genuinely care

**Step 3: Be specific with outreach**
- Don't send generic "I love your content, let's collaborate"
- Reference specific videos, moments, or jokes
- Shows you actually watch their content

**Step 4: Connect the dots to your product**
- Show alignment, not just a transaction
- Demonstrate product-audience fit

**Step 5: Acknowledge value and signal willingness to pay early**
- Lead with incentives
- Show examples of similar apps making money (Sensor Tower data)
- Give them a mental model of future potential

# 🧠 Frameworks & Lessons

---

## Mental Models

> 🧩 **Distribution > Product:** "The only thing wrong with your app is that nobody was seeing it." Building is cool, but distribution is the hard part.

> 🧩 **Quality over Quantity:** One high-quality influencer with skin in the game beats 20 random partnerships. Three story-driven videos per month converts better than 100 viral attempts.

> 🧩 **Skin in the Game:** Profit-sharing aligns incentives. The influencer thinks like a co-founder, not a contractor.

> 🧩 **Go Global, Not Just US:** Partnered with a Colombian influencer instead of US-based. Opens up untapped markets.

> 🧩 **Add Social Handles to Your App:** Content creators will find your app and reach out if it's interesting. Makes inbound partnership possible.

---

## The Gold

> ✨ **"The only thing wrong with your app is that nobody was seeing it."**

> ✨ **"Quality over quantity - both in influencers and in content."**

> ✨ **"Don't just limit distribution to the US. There are great content creators everywhere."**
