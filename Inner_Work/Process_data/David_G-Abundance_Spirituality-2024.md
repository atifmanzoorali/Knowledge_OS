# Why Isn't Everyone Receiving Limitless Abundance?

**Teacher:** David G. | **Topic:** Abundance & Spirituality | **Category:** Kabbalah

---

# 📝 Full Context

David G. is a spiritual teacher and entrepreneur who spent decades studying and teaching Kabbalah—the universal spiritual laws behind manifestation, fulfillment, and wealth. He took a vow of poverty at 19, joined a nonprofit, and taught Kabbalah globally for 25 years before co-founding Mary Ruth Organics, now one of the world's leading wellness brands. In this conversation with Emily, he explains why so many spiritual people struggle with money, and how manifestation isn't about asking for more—it's about becoming the vessel that can actually hold it. The core teaching: your soul came to "earn" the light, not receive it for free. Challenges are the puzzle you're meant to solve.

---

# 💎 Core Wisdom

## The Central Insight

> 🧠 **Manifestation isn't about asking for more—it's about becoming the vessel that can hold what you desire.**

Your soul didn't request a ready-made puzzle. The challenges in your life (relationships, abundance, health) are the puzzle you're meant to solve. If you got everything effortlessly, you'd feel "bread of shame"—the discomfort of receiving without earning. Every area where energy is blocked is simply a place where your vessel needs to expand.

---

## Key Principles

### 🎯 The Vessel Analogy

> 📖 *"If you're a 60-watt bulb and your soulmate is a thousand watts of energy, finding them would implode your vessel. You couldn't enjoy it or hold it."*

Your capacity to receive determines what enters your life. Growth comes from transforming yourself, not from praying for more. The sages call this transforming your "vessel."

---

### 🎯 Two Forces: Creator & Vessel

> 📖 *"There's only two forces: the creator (the light of the creator) and the vessel (the desire to receive that energy)."*

Kabbalah explains all of life—love, soulmates, healing, consciousness, manifestation—as the interaction between fulfillment and desire. Your job is to create the vessel that holds the light.

---

### 🎯 Certainty + Happiness = Miracles

> 📖 *"To the extent that a person has certainty, certainty that everything is good... certainty that all of this is an illusion and you can get to a place of happiness—certainty plus happiness already elevates you to the frequency that is similar to the creator."*

When you have doubts, anxiety, and fear, you can never receive the miracle. Your daily practice: shift from worry to certainty that everything is serving your growth.

---

### 🎯 Belief Systems Determine Your Vessel Size

> 📖 *"The number one predictor of what your vessel can hold is what you believe you deserve to hold."*

Limited beliefs: "I don't deserve wealth." Competing beliefs: "I want to grow but I hate employees." Both block abundance. Your subconscious beliefs override conscious desires.

---

# 🛠️ Practical Application

## Daily Practices

- ✅ **Morning Repogramming:** Spend 10-30 minutes shifting from worry to certainty. Say phrases opposite your limited beliefs: *"Men are safe. Men are amazing. I am worthy of abundance."*

- ✅ **Certainty Practice:** Before leaving your house, never feel like you're "missing something." That energy attracts chaos.

- ✅ **The Opposite Mantra:** Create mantras that contradict your competing beliefs. Repeat for 10 minutes daily on walks.

## Reflection Questions

> 🤔 *What limiting belief do I hold about deserving abundance?*

> 🤔 *What competing belief is canceling out my desires? (e.g., "I want growth but hate employees")*

> 🤔 *Am I acting like the creator—or from lack, doubt, and fear?*
